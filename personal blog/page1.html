Honey Home

Jamal Amanova

Contour Media is a creative product design studio in Connecticut operated by Jamal Amanova, a confident and creative Product Designer, Graphic Design Instructor, and Experienced Artist with a demonstrated history of organizing multiple Fine Art Exhibitions across the USA and Central Asia. She has been traveling, painting, and teaching traditional approaches in art making for the past 15 years, which helped her to discover her strong analytical and problem-solving skills. Jamal has decided to help people with her abilities in User-Centered Design and Product Thinking while empowering her visual communication, problem-solving, and critical thinking skills through various technical approaches at the same time. Her strong combination of design thinking helped many start-up companies in solving complex problems. As Tim Brown, the leading voice on the value of design thinking in business and society, says, Design can help to improve our lives in the present. Design thinking can help us chart a path into the future. Change by Design: How Design Thinking Transforms Organizations and Inspires Innovation She keeps on upgrading her technical skills and experimental approaches in Design. Jamal is passionate about UX/UI design and eager to learn more about new techniques in Visual Communication while working on becoming a design professional and educator.
